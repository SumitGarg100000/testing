All Features Explained

  • Personalized AI CAs
    Aap alag-alag expert AI CAs bana sakte hain. Har AI ki aap `Name`, `Age`, `Gender`, `Act Like` (Friend/Professional) aur `Expertise` (GST, Tax, Audit, etc.) set kar sakte hain. Isse aap har kaam ke liye ek dedicated expert bana sakte hain.
  • Custom Expertise & Behaviour
    Yeh ek power-user feature hai. 'Expertise' chunn ne ke alawa, aap is box mein AI ko khaas instructions de sakte hain. Yeh AI ke default behaviour ko override kar deta hai.
    Kaise use karein: Is box mein likhein...
    • "Always answer in bullet points."
    • "Act like a senior partner reviewing a file."
    • "Always cite relevant sections from the Income Tax Act."
    • "Be very brief and to the point."
  • Advanced File Analysis (PDF, Excel, JSON, etc.)
    Aap .pdf, .xlsx, .docx, .json, aur .jpg/.png (images) file upload kar sakte hain. AI uss file ke content ko padhega aur aapke sawaalon ka jawaab *sirf uss file ke data* se dega. Yeh client's balance sheet, bank statement, ya lambe notifications ko analyze karne ke liye perfect hai.
  • 'Latest Updates' Dashboard
    Main screen par yeh button aapko ek dashboard par le jaata hai. Yahaan aap "Update Cards" bana sakte hain. Har card mein aap ek topic/keyword (jaise: "latest gst council meeting updates") daal sakte hain. Jab bhi aap uss card par click karenge, AI aapke liye Google se real-time mein latest news search karke laayega.
  • Group Chat & AI Debates
    Aap ek 'Group' bana kar usmein apne banaye hue multiple AI experts (jaise 'Auditor' aur 'Tax Consultant') ko add kar sakte hain. Jab aap group mein koi complex sawaal poochenge (jaise: "Related party transaction ka audit aur tax perspective batao"), toh dono AI experts apne-apne field ke hisaab se jawaab denge, jisse aapko ek virtual debate ka fayda milega.
  • The 'Skip' (➡️) Button
    Yeh button *sirf* Group Chat mein dikhta hai. Jab aap Skip button (➡️) dabate hain, toh aap AI ko aapas mein baat karne ka mauka dete hain. Yeh feature debate ko aage badhane ya bina aapke input ke conversation ko naturally flow karne ke liye hai.
  • Up-to-Date & Reliable Information
    Yeh AI 2023 ke puraane data par dependent nahi hai. Jab aap koi tax slab, due date, ya notification poochte hain, toh AI real-time Google Search ka istemal karta hai. Yeh reliable sources (jaise incometaxindia.gov.in, gst.gov.in, mca.gov.in) ko priority deta hai, taaki aapko hamesha correct aur up-to-date information mile.
  • Google Drive Sync & Backup
    'Connect to Drive' par click karke aap apna saara data (chats, profile, AI characters) apne personal Google Drive par backup kar sakte hain. Agar aap app delete karte hain ya phone change karte hain, toh bhi naye login par aapka saara data waapas aa jaayega.
  • AI Block Feature (Realistic Personality)
    Yeh AI ek virtual human ki tarah design kiya gaya hai. Iska goal helpful aur professional rehna hai. Lekin, agar user ka behaviour inappropriate (jaise abusive language ya personal attacks) hota hai, toh AI ko bura lag sakta hai aur woh user ko block kar sakta hai. Yeh ek realistic feature hai. Note: User AI ko block nahi kar sakta.

How This App Saves a CA's Time (Use Cases)

Use Case 1: Document Review & Summary

Problem: Ek client ne 50-page ki bank statement PDF bheji hai ya ek naya circular aaya hai.
App Solution: PDF ko upload karein aur seedha poochein: "Summarize this circular in 5 bullet points" ya "Find all transactions above Rs 2,00,000".
Time Saved: 1-2 ghante ka manual reading time 2 minute mein complete.

Use Case 2: Quick Data Analysis

Problem: Client ne Excel (.xlsx) ya JSON format mein data bheja hai.
App Solution: File upload karein aur poochein: "Convert this JSON data into a clean table" ya "Is Excel file mein total sales kitni hai?".
Time Saved: Data ko import karne, filter/pivot lagane ka 15-30 minute ka time bachta hai.

Use Case 3: Staying Updated

Problem: Roz 5 alag-alag websites (GST, MCA, IT Portal) check karni padti hain.
App Solution: "Latest Updates" feature mein 5 cards bana dein (Ek GST, ek MCA, etc.). Subah bas 5 card par click karein aur aapko 2 minute mein poore din ki real-time updates mil jaayengi.
Time Saved: Daily 30-45 minutes ka browsing time bachta hai.

Use Case 4: Complex Problem Solving

Problem: Ek complex situation hai jismein Audit aur Tax dono ka angle hai.
App Solution: Ek 'Group Chat' banayein jismein aapka 'Auditor AI' aur 'Tax AI' dono ho. Apni query daalein. Aapko dono experts se viewpoint mil jaayenge, jisse aapki decision-making strong hogi. Access to multiple expert opinions without consulting multiple colleagues.
Time Saved: Research aur colleagues se consultation ka kaafi time bachta hai.

Practical Steps (Hinglish Guide)

1. Profile & Login

App kholne par Secret Code aur Reference Code daalein (jo developer se milega). Login ke baad, top-right mein profile icon (👤) se apna naam/photo set karein. 'Connect to Drive' par click karke Google Drive sync zaroor on karein.

2. Pehla AI CA Banana

Main screen par "+ New AI" par click karein. Naam (jaise: "My Tax Expert") aur 'Expertise' (jaise: Tax Consultant) chunein. Agar aapko expert se hamesha bullet points mein jawaab chahiye, toh 'Custom Expertise & Behaviour' box mein likh dein: "Always reply in bullet points."

3. Group Debate Karwana

Do alag AI (Ek 'GST Expert', ek 'Audit Expert') banayein. Phir "+ New Group" par click karke un dono ko add karein. Ab group mein sawaal poochein. Agar AI aapas mein baat na karein, toh Skip (➡️) button dabayein.

4. Roz Updated Rehna

Main screen par "Latest Updates" button par click karein. "+ Create Card" par click karein. Naam dein "GST Updates" aur Topic/Keywords mein likhein "latest gst notification cbic". Ab roz is card par click karke 2 minute mein saari nayi updates paayein.

Aap app ko explore karein, koi bhi dikkat aaye toh upar diye gaye support number par WhatsApp karein.
